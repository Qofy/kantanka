New Brand Kantanka Car

Kantanka Automobile Company is a Ghanaian automotive manufacturer known for producing a range of vehicles, including SUVs, pickup trucks, and luxury cars. Established by Kwadwo Safo Kantanka, the company emphasizes African innovation and craftsmanship, incorporating modern technology while also reflecting traditional Ghanaian values. Kantanka cars are designed to meet local needs and preferences, contributing to the growth of the automotive industry in Ghana and promoting self-reliance in manufacturing on the African continent.
